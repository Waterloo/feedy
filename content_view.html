Grumpy Cat will be the world's most miserable comic book hero

Following deals for merchandise, endorsements and even a Lifetime Original Christmas movie, the hardest working cat on the Internet is back

And this time, Grumpy Cat is a comic book hero.

See also: Meet the artist who made a Grumpy Cat bust out of bacon

Image: Dynamite

Grumpy will work with Dynamite Entertainment to bring her famous scowl to the pages of comic books, graphic novels and comic strips. And this isn't her first foray into the world of literature, either; she's already the "author" of two New York Times bestsellers

The Internet-famous cat and her brother Pokey will debut in comic form with a three-issue miniseries out this fall, which will be bound into a hardcover graphic novel available later this year. Grumpy will also appear in daily digital comic strips that will appear online this summer. Read more...
